Hotdog

This is an Appetizer in America

Ingredients

  • Meat trimming
  • paprika
  • salt
  • garlic

Directions

  • Place the meat and fat on a rimmed baking sheet
  • In a small bowl, add salt, paprika, granulated garlic, black pepper, onion powder, and Instacure #1 and stir to combine.
  • Nest a large mixing bowl in a bowl filled with ice.
  • Stuff the sausage into the sheep casings and twist into links.
